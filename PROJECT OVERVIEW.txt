PROJECT OVERVIEW
Project Name

AI Fashion Pose & Style Finder

Project Objective

Build an AI-powered web application that helps users find aesthetic reference photos based on their own outfit, tattoos, accessories, and desired look.

Instead of searching Pinterest manually, users upload their outfit and tattoo images, and the AI analyzes them to recommend visually similar photos from Pinterest (or another image source) that can be recreated in real life.

The AI should understand the complete aesthetic rather than simply matching colors.
